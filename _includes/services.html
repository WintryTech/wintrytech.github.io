Our Story

At the heart of Wintry Tech AS lies compassion for our people and passion for our clients. We are a technology consultancy company based in Oslo, Norway. We provide digital consultancy services such as full-stack development, system architecture and business advisory.

We specialize in development technologies such as React, React Native (mobile app development), TypeScript, Angular and the Microsoft stack, including high-class exceptional expertise in C#, Microsoft Azure and .NET/.NET Core.

Our Goal

Our goal is simple. We believe in quality work and we are passionate about our craft, dedicated to building you solid, secure, high-end and resilient systems.

Our motto is: Compassion for our people, passion for our clients.
